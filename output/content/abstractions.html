Improved Comprehension

Raw data can be overwhelming and difficult to interpret. Abstraction condenses it into a more concise and understandable format, making it easier for humans to grasp key insights and patterns.

Reduced Cognitive Load

Processing intricate details of raw data takes mental effort. Abstraction simplifies the information, minimizing cognitive load and enabling faster, more intuitive understanding

Enhanced Decision-Making:

By revealing trends and relationships buried within raw data, higher-level abstractions provide a clearer basis for informed decision-making.

Increased Communication:

Abstractions simplify data sharing and communication. Instead of bogging down others with raw data, you can present concise summaries and visualizations that convey essential information more effectively.
