"Exploring AI: Chatbots, Virtual Assistants & Ticketing Systems"

The article provides an overview of AI-powered agents including chatbots, virtual assistants, and automated ticketing systems. Chatbots are designed to interact with humans in their natural languages, virtual assistants understand voice commands and perform tasks, while automated ticketing systems manage and streamline the ticketing process, offering 24/7 support.

AI-Powered Agents Description
Chatbots
Chatbots are AI-powered software designed to interact with humans in their natural languages. These chatbots are usually conversational agents, programmed to conduct online chat conversation via text or text-to-speech, in lieu of providing direct contact with a live human agent.
Virtual Assistants
Virtual Assistants are AI-powered systems that understand natural language voice commands and performs tasks for the user. They can perform tasks or services for an individual based on commands or questions. They use AI technologies, like machine learning and voice recognition, to provide customer service.
Automated Ticketing Systems
Automated Ticketing Systems are AI-powered systems that manage and streamline the ticketing process. They can automatically sort, organize, and prioritize tickets, making it easier for customer service representatives to respond to customer inquiries. They offer 24/7 support, ensuring that customer inquiries are addressed promptly and efficiently.
